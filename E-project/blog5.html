When is the Best Time of Year to Look for New Citroen Vans
for Sale?

For businesses and individuals considering a fleet upgrade or a new purchase, timing can be
everything. When it comes to finding the best deals on new Citroen vans for sale, knowing the
right time of year to start your search can lead to substantial savings and a smoother buying
experience. This article will guide you through the ideal times to look for new Citroen vans,
ensuring you get the best value for your investment.

End of the Financial Year

One of the prime times to search for new Citroen vans for sale is at the end of the financial year.
Dealerships are often looking to clear out existing stock to make room for new models,
leading to significant discounts and attractive financing options. This period typically falls around
March or April, depending on the fiscal calendar of the dealership’s country. Purchasing during
this time can lead to savings, as dealerships are more likely to negotiate to meet annual sales targets.

New Model Release

Another strategic time to buy is just before or after a new Citroen van model is released.
Dealers will often reduce prices on current stock to clear space for the latest models.
Keeping an eye on Citroen’s release schedule can help you time your purchase to capitalise
on these deals. However, if you’re interested in the latest features and designs, waiting for the
new model release could be more beneficial.
